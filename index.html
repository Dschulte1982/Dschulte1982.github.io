Dust 'Em Off

Dust 'Em Off is social platform that allows hobby ethusiasts to share and display the collections they love. This scalable application is comprised entirely of user-generated content, designed to grow exponentially with increased interaction. If Ebay is the go-to resource to view the collectibles people want to part with, Dust 'Em Off is poised to become the place to view the collectibles they love.

Batmanhood

No commissions, no hassles, and no actual stocks or money - Batmanhood, your comprehensive resource for pretending to be a major player in the stock market!

Meatium

Meatium is a full-stack food-based news and blogging application inspired and modeled after Medium. This application was a collaborative effort with two other aspiring developers, flexing our JavaScripting muscles with real-time event handling and an Express back-end.
